Admin Home

To start using CalMapIt, configure your Trips first. Once you select your Trip, you will configure Events and Place to Go for your trip.

Locations are shared across trips.

Helpful tips:

  • Use the Home tab to create helpful announcement for users to stay up-to-date.
  • It's good to configure Locations early in the process since you'll need to assign Locations for each trip.
  • After creating events, you can always go back and change the start time.
